L'indécis

Arnaud Bresson

Musician/Producer at Chillhop Music

In 2011, after finding himself drawn to the hip-hop scene in Grenoble, France, L’indécis [Arnaud Bresson] started his formative career as a music producer.

Classically trained as a guitarist, L’indécis seamlessly balances easy listening with quality arrangements, similar to inspiring peers like FKJ, Kiefer and Tom Misch.

Over the last five years, L’indécis has released multiple projects with significant acclaim through Chillhop Records. This ongoing relationship between producer and label has solidified him as a household name within the lo-fi hip hop community.
